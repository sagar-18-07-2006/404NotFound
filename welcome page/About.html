Project Overview

Nationale-Hospital is a conceptual digitalplatformcreatedduringahealthcarehackathontodemonstratehow technology can improvehospitalappointmentsystemsand patient accessto care.

This prototype showcaseskeyfeatureslikeunifiedappointment booking,queue management, anddigital health records -alldesigned to reducewaittimes andimprove healthcareefficiency.

Hackathon Focus Areas

  • Digitaltransformationofhealthcaresystems
  • Patient-centereddesign principles
  • Scalablearchitecturefornationwide implementation
